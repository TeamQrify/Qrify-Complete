Who We Are

Our Journey

QRIFY began with a simple idea: to transform how people discover and enjoy food in their cities. Founded in 2018 by three food enthusiasts who were frustrated with the disconnect between amazing local restaurants and hungry customers, we set out to build a platform that would bring them together.

What started as a small project in a single neighborhood has now expanded to serve over 200 cities, connecting millions of food lovers with over 25,000 restaurant partners. Our mission remains the same: to ensure no great meal goes undiscovered.

Today, FoodFusion is more than just a food delivery service. We're a comprehensive food platform that helps people discover new cuisines, supports local restaurants in reaching new customers, and creates economic opportunities for delivery partners.

Our Mission & Values

We're on a mission to transform how people connect with technology by providing seamless access to the best dining experiences while empowering restaurant partners and supporting local communities.
